The Service may use external services for analytics or other purposes. Information handling by such external services is governed by each provider's policies.

The Service uses the Mapbox SDK to provide map display and location-based features. Under Mapbox's privacy policy, the Mapbox SDK may collect and process the following information in a de-identified or non-directly identifying manner:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers (such as device ID and advertising ID)
  • Location data (such as latitude and longitude)
  • Telemetry usage data (such as crash and usage metrics)

This information is used for map service delivery, service quality improvement, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting. Mapbox's published materials also describe privacy safeguards such as session ID rotation and limits on IP address retention.
